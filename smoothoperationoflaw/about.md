# About SmoothOperationofLaw

Legal systems are complex by design — but understanding your rights and obligations shouldn't require a law degree or a $400/hour attorney consultation.

**SmoothOperationofLaw** was built on a simple belief: everyone deserves to understand the legal documents they receive, the rights they hold, and the processes that affect their lives.

---

## What We Do

We combine structured legal education with AI-powered document analysis to help people:

- **Understand documents** they receive — contracts, demand letters, court filings, lease agreements
- **Learn the law** that applies to their situation, in plain language
- **Research their rights** quickly and accurately
- **Prepare** to work more effectively with an attorney when they need one

---

## Our Approach

### Plain Language First
Every lesson is written for people without legal training. We define terms, use real examples, and avoid jargon wherever possible. When legal terms are necessary, we explain them.

### AI-Assisted, Human-Reviewed
Our AI tools are powered by the Gemini API and are designed to help you understand documents and research questions. All course content is reviewed by licensed attorneys before publication.

### Education, Not Advice
We are an education platform, not a law firm. We help you understand — but for advice about your specific legal situation, you need a licensed attorney in your jurisdiction. We make it easy to find one when you're ready.

---

## Technology

Our document analysis and Q&A features are built using the [Google Gemini API](https://ai.google.dev/), which provides powerful language understanding capabilities. We use:

- **Gemini 2.5 Flash** for fast, accurate document analysis
- **Structured output** to extract key clauses and deadlines from contracts
- **Grounding** with Google Search for up-to-date legal research
