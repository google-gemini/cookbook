# Certification Pathways

SmoothOperationofLaw™ offers three professional certification levels, plus an invitation-only Elite Practitioner designation. Each builds on the previous, culminating in the highest credential in strategic trust and IP enforcement practice.

---

## CATM — Certified Advanced Trust Manager

**Prerequisite:** Completion of Parts I–IV (Chapters 1–12)

The CATM certification establishes your foundational mastery of strategic trust management, the Federal Arbitration Act, intellectual property integration, and practical enforcement tools.

### What CATM Covers
- Strategic Trust Management Framework (Part I)
- FAA mastery and arbitration clause design (Part II)
- IP asset protection and licensing (Part II)
- Risk management and crisis protocols (Part II)
- Document templates and case study analysis (Part IV)

### CATM Requirements
1. Complete Parts I–IV (all chapters and assessments)
2. Pass the CATM Certification Examination
3. Complete the Ethics Assessment
4. Agree to the Professional Code of Conduct

### CATM Benefits
- Professional certification credential
- Access to the CATM template library
- Alumni network membership
- Continuing education credit tracking

**Included in:** Foundation Level ($2,997) and all higher tiers.

## CATM+ — Certified Advanced Trust Manager Plus

**Prerequisite:** CATM Certification + Completion of Parts V–VI (Chapters 13–28)

CATM+ extends your expertise into international IP protection, ICSID proceedings, bilateral investment treaty enforcement, and advanced international trust structures.

### What CATM+ Adds
- Global IP landscape and territorial rights (Part V)
- Cross-border enforcement and international treaty utilization (Part V)
- Foreign sovereign immunity doctrines (Part V)
- ICSID and BIT enforcement (Part VI)
- International commercial arbitration institutions (Part VI)
- Cross-border asset recovery and collection (Part VI)
- International trust structures and tax optimization (Part VI)

### CATM+ Requirements
1. Hold CATM Certification
2. Complete Parts V–VI (all chapters and assessments)
3. Pass the CATM+ Certification Examination
4. Complete the Practical Assessment

**Included in:** Advanced Practitioner ($7,997) and higher tiers.
