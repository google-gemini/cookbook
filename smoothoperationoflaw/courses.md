# Courses

Our courses are designed for people without legal backgrounds. Each one walks you through a legal topic step by step, with plain-language explanations, real examples, and AI-powered Q&A to help you apply what you learn.

---

## Contracts & Agreements

### Understanding Contracts
*4 lessons · Beginner*

Learn how to read any contract before you sign it. Covers the essential elements of a valid contract, common clauses to watch for, red flags, and how to negotiate basic terms.

**You'll learn:**
- What makes a contract legally binding
- How to identify payment terms, termination clauses, and liability limits
- Common tactics to spot and avoid
- When to walk away — and how
