**Section 1 — Legal Framework (25%)**

**Section 3 — Practical Application (30%)**

- Post-judgment collection strategy

**Section 4 — Advanced Strategy (20%)**
- Multi-domain warfare coordination
- Intelligence operations and counter-intelligence
- Psychological warfare ethics and limits

### Capstone Project

The capstone project requires you to design a complete enforcement campaign for a hypothetical (or real, anonymized) fact pattern involving a government entity and trust-held IP. The capstone must include:

1. Target analysis memorandum
2. Immunity pathway scoring matrix
3. Arbitration clause review and amendment recommendation
4. Commercial activity documentation plan
5. Phase-by-phase implementation timeline
6. Template selection and customization rationale
7. Risk assessment and mitigation plan
8. Success metrics and validation criteria

### Oral Interview with Lord Ramon

CGIPE candidates complete a 60-minute oral examination with Lord Ramon covering:
- The candidate's enforcement philosophy
- Application of the system to a novel fact pattern
- Professional responsibility and ethical judgment
- Career and practice development plans
